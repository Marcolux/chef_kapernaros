Chef Nikolaos Kapernaros

In every dish I craft, the essence of my homeland whispers, telling tales of my culture and family traditions. My culinary journey is a homage to the land and people that raised me, a celebration of bountiful produce, vibrant flavors and enduring heritage. This is more than cooking; it's a journey back to my roots, a path I tread with every ingredient I choose and every recipe I create. Welcome to my story of passion, tradition and the unbreakable bond between culture and cuisine.

Charity Events

Participating in charity events is something very important to me, throughout my career and my life. I feel incredibly fortunate to be able to help others with the support of my employers and colleagues.
I am member of Chicago Chefs Cook, a living organization of Chefs in one of the best cities in the World. It is energized by building relationships in the community with the common purpose of contributing to those in need.
I am also a proud member of World Central Kitchen Chef Corps, feeding humanity, feeding hope. WCK is first to the Frontline, providing fresh meals in response to humanitarian, climate, and community crises.
